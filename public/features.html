Features

Customizable travel planning

Our website allows you to travel the world in their own way. By entering the starting and ending points, you can answer selected questions in our questionnaire that will ultimately calculate the best route for you!

Accurate route calculation

Our algorithm takes into account various different factors and variables such as means of traveling, traffic, weather and road closures and so on, all with the objective of providing you with the most efficient travel route!

“Ease of use” interface

Our website is designed to be easy to navigate, understand and use, so that people of any demographic can have a smooth and seamless travel planning experience!

Personalized Recommendations

Based on the answers given on the questionnaire, our algorithm can provide you with personalized recommendations in your route such as attractions, restaurants and other points of interest!
