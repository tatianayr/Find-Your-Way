About us

Welcome to our website!

“Find your way” is a travel website designed to facilitate and streamline the planning process before every trip, while guaranteeing the user the best traveling route for them.

The platform has a great focus on reliability and user-friendliness, so that anyone of any demographic can visit us and efficiently plan their travels with great ease.

This website is part of a college project for the “Computer Science/Engineering” course, and was developed by Camila Madatali and Tatiana Yermachenkava.

Thank you for visiting our website!
